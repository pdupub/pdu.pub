Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2025

Thank you for using LingRain. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

LingRain is committed to respecting your privacy. We do not actively collect any personally identifiable information.

The app only records anonymous learning-related data locally on your device, such as:

  • Number and duration of audio plays
  • Learning progress in courses
  • Customized settings (e.g., playback preferences, notification time ranges)

This data is stored only on your device and used solely to optimize your experience.

2. Third-Party Services

LingRain does not integrate or use any third-party advertising or analytics services. Your learning data and preferences are never uploaded to servers or shared with third parties.

3. Local Notifications

The app may optionally use local notifications to gently remind you to continue your listening practice. Notification times can be freely configured in the settings. All notifications are generated locally on your device without network transmission.

4. Permission Requests

To provide a complete experience, LingRain may request the following permissions:

  • Notification Permission: Used to send gentle learning reminders.
  • Photo Access Permission (only requested when changing your profile picture, solely to select an image; the app does not access other photos).

Permissions are used solely for their intended purpose and only after obtaining your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

Your data remains entirely on your device and is never uploaded or shared externally. We continually strive to implement best practices to protect your data from unauthorized access.

6. Your Choices

You can revoke notification or photo access permissions at any time via your device settings. When you delete the app, all locally stored data will be removed as well.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or need assistance regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
