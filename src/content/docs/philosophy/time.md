In PDU, Time is not a flowing river but a cognitive tool used to organize "Changes."

### Matter, Space, and Change
The universe consists only of **Matter** and **Space**. "Change" is an inherent property of matter in space. 

### From Change to Event
1. **Change:** The objective movement of matter.
2. **Event:** A cognitive "package" created by an observer to simplify complex changes.
3. **Time:** The measurement of the partial order of these events.

### Why this matters for PDU
Since there is no "Universal Clock" in a decentralized network, the protocol defines **Identity** through a **Total Order** of events. 
- If a publisher creates two conflicting events (a "fork"), they are violating the fundamental nature of time within their own identity.
- In P2P space, "Now" is simply the boundary of your current information horizon.
