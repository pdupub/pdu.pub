### The Concept of "Horizon" (视界)
Existence is not an absolute switch. In ParaDigi Universe, something exists for an observer only when it enters that observer's visible horizon.

- **Subjective existence:** an identity exists to you only when you have received and validated enough of its history.
- **The filter:** your horizon is defined by your trust rules. If you block a publisher, they disappear from your local universe.

### Entropy and Life
We define **life** as an individual that maintains internal order through interaction with the outside world.

- **Information as energy:** in a social network, useful information lowers uncertainty for the receiver
- **Spam as heat:** meaningless information increases noise and cognitive cost

ParaDigi Universe lets every consumer build a local heat shield through filtering, preserving mental and informational order without demanding universal censorship.
