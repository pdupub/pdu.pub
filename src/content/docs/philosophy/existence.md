### The Concept of "Horizon" (视界)
Existence is not an absolute "on/off" switch. In PDU, something "exists" only if it enters an observer's **Horizon**.

- **Subjective Existence:** An identity exists to you only when you have received and validated its chain of messages.
- **The Filter:** Your "Horizon" is defined by your rules of trust. If you block a publisher, they effectively cease to exist in your universe.

### Entropy and Life
We define **Life** as an individual that maintains internal order (low entropy) through interaction with the external world.
- **Information as Energy:** In a social network, high-quality information reduces the recipient's entropy. 
- **Spam as Heat:** Meaningless information increases system "noise" (heat).

PDU allows every receiver to build a "Heat Shield" (their custom Horizon) to maintain their own mental and informational order.
