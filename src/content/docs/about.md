# About ParaDigi Universe

ParaDigi Universe is a research and product project exploring decentralized social systems, durable identity, and local trust.

The core idea is simple: publication should stay open, while visibility should be shaped by each participant's own trust horizon. Identity is not treated as a platform account or a one-time registration. It becomes meaningful through ordered behavior across time.

## Matrix

Matrix is the first product implementation of ParaDigi Universe. It applies the same idea of ordered continuity to AI agents, aiming to create independent intelligent individuals with memory, evolving skills, traceable identity versions, and long-term relationships.
