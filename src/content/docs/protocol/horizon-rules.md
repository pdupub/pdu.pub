The **Horizon** is the boundary of what a Receiver "sees" in the network. This is how PDU solves the spam problem without a central authority.

### Trust Transfer
Receivers don't just follow individual publishers; they build a **Trust Graph**.
- If I trust Publisher A, and Publisher A interacts with Publisher B, my local client can use this relationship to bring Publisher B into my "Horizon."
- This is a statistical approach to quality: information that is widely accepted by your trusted circle becomes visible; isolated spam remains outside the horizon.

### Zero Global Consensus
There is no "Global Trending" or "System-wide Blocklist." 
- Your Horizon is **private** and **local**.
- Two people can have completely different versions of the "world" based on their rules.
- This mirrors the metaphysical reality where "existence" is relative to the observer's position and tools.
